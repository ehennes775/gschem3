calculate_offset

Description:

protected override double calculate_offset ()

Calculate the offset of the grip

The offset of the grip ensures that grips don't overlap.

A positive offset indicates the grip lies on the outside of the shape. A negative offset indicates the grip lies on the inside of the shape. With an offset of zero, the grip lies on the circumference of the shape.
